Projects

Throughout his tenure in TUD Tallaght Campus, Barry has been involved in software development projects to demonstrate his ability. These projects were both in collaboration with other peers and individually by Barry himself.

Author Collaboration Website

The Author Collaboration Website was a three person project undetaken by Barry and his peers during the second year of his Computing course. This website was built using Play! framework to create a fullstack web application that allowed its users to create literary content in a collaborative fashion and discuss through forums topics ranging from story ideas to off topic chat. A community shop was also provided through the website to publish and sell community works with customers.

Lixer

Lixer is an ongoing web development project being developed during the final year of his course on an individual basis that will allow family members to video call elderly relatives with a special focus on ease of use for the elderly. This application will also enable family members to send media files to their elderly relatives in the forms of audio files, images and videos. Lixer is being built with a combination of ReactJS, WebRTC and google Firebase and eventually will also be offered as an android application.
